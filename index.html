    Conserving The Wild: Koala And Its Habitat

    The Australian Koala Foundation (AKF) is the principal non-profit, non-government organisation dedicated to the effective management and conservation of the Koala and its habitat. Our main goals are to refine and update the Koala Habitat Atlas mapping of all wild Koala habitat and to get the Koala Protection Act enacted to ensure Koalas and their habitat are protected.

    NURTURING KOALAS

    The Australian Koala Foundation tenderly cradles the future of koalas in the nurturing hands of conservation, breathing life into the eucalyptus-scented lungs of these arboreal marsupials, as we gently shepherd them back to the verdant embrace of their forest homes.

    PROJECT SAFE KOALA

    The Australian Koala Foundation sweeps into action like a vigilant guardian, plucking vulnerable koalas from the jaws of peril and swathing them in the sanctuary of rehabilitation and care.

    EDUCATING MINDS ABOUT KOALAS

    The Australian Koala Foundation illuminates minds with the sparkle of knowledge, spreading awareness about koalas like seeds of wisdom taking root across communities.
